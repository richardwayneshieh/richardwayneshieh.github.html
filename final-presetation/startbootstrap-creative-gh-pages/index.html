Which kind of Chinese food is most popular?

Taking Guangdong as the center, the main cuisines of major provinces are listed in the circle. Try to find people's favorite local cuisine, and estimate which region are immigrants from through food preferences.
The results show that people like Sichuan cuisine most. Sichuan cuisine, Hunan cuisine and Guangdong cuisine account for most of the catering industry in Shenzhen.


Where do people come from?

Remove data interference from the most popular cuisines.
From Fujian region to the distant regions of Jiangsu,Zhejiang,Anhui and Hubei provinces, to farther northeast, northwest and Yungui regions. Data suggest that the Northeast and Northwest cuisine are more popular in Shenzhen. We suspect that there are more people from these places in Shenzhen.

The chart statistics several major fast-food brands in Shenzhen, including KFC,MacDonald,PizzaHut,YongHe, and combines the number of non-registered population in different districts.
